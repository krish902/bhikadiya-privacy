Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 23-12-2025

Bhikadiya Family App ("we", "our", "us") is a private community-based mobile application designed to manage family, village, and community-related information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it while using the app.

  • Personal Information: First name, middle name, last name, gender, date of birth.
  • Contact Details: Mobile number, email address.
  • Location Details: Village, city, state, country.
  • Family Information: Family membersâ€™ details, relationships, and family head identification.
  • Community Details: Blood group, education, occupation, business or job information.
  • Donation Information: Fund donation history, purpose, and status.
  • Abroad / NRI Information: Work sector, designation, career details, city of residence.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To verify users through village representatives (Gam Pratinidhi).
  • To maintain family and village directories.
  • To manage blood donor listings and community services.
  • To record and display donation history transparently.
  • To allow admins to manage community-related approvals.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • Education and occupation details are used only to understand community background and are not used for profiling, scoring, or employment decisions.

3. User Approval & Roles

User registrations may be reviewed and approved by authorized administrators or village representatives. Different roles such as User, Admin, and Gam Pratinidhi have different access levels.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

  • Data is visible only within the community as per your role.
  • Admins and authorized representatives can view data for verification purposes.
  • We may use limited third-party services only for essential functions such as sending verification messages or basic performance monitoring. No personal data is shared for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data. Passwords are encrypted, and access is restricted based on user roles.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account remains active or as required for community management purposes.

7. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account at any time through the app or via our official deletion request page. Once approved, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.
